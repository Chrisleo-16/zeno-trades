# Sniper Trading Journal - Quick Start Guide

## Get Started in 5 Minutes

### 1. Sign Up
- Go to `/` (splash screen)
- Click "Create Account"
- Enter email and password
- That's it!

### 2. Log Your First Trade
- Click "Log New Trade" on dashboard
- **Complete the Pre-Trade Checklist** (all 10 items!)
- Fill in trade details:
  - Pair (EUR/USD)
  - Type (Long/Short)
  - Entry Price
  - Exit Price
  - Quantity
  - Status (Pending/Win/Loss/Breakeven)
- Click "Save Trade"

### 3. Get Strategy Recommendations
- Go to "Strategies" page
- Click "Get AI Recommendations"
- Read the 3 recommended strategies
- Click "Use This Strategy" to add one
- View your strategies in the list

### 4. Learn & Improve
- Go to "Learn & Coach"
- Start with "Psychology 101" (most important)
- Then try "London Breakout Strategy Setup"
- Read case studies to learn from real trades
- Mark modules as completed

### 5. Check Your Analytics
- Go to "Analytics"
- See your win rate, P&L, discipline score
- Review equity curve and trade distribution
- Get AI insights on improvements

## Essential Features Explained

### Pre-Trade Checklist (Do NOT Skip!)
This is the most important feature. You MUST complete all 10 items before entering ANY trade:

1. ✓ Identified clear entry signal
2. ✓ Risk/reward ratio 1:2+
3. ✓ Stop loss set
4. ✓ Take profit set
5. ✓ Chart timeframe correct
6. ✓ Right trading session
7. ✓ Position size correct
8. ✓ No conflicting trades
9. ✓ Emotions calm
10. ✓ Understand exit plan

**Why?** This single gate prevents 90% of losing trades. Follow the system, not your emotions.

### AI Strategy Recommendations
You'll receive 3 strategies based on:
- Your trading experience level
- Your risk tolerance
- Your preferred market session
- Your account size goals

Each recommendation shows:
- **Confidence %** - How well it matches your profile
- **Why this strategy** - 4 reasons it fits you
- **Key rules** - 5-6 specific trading rules
- **Risk level** - Low/Medium/High

### Win Rate vs. Discipline
- **Win Rate**: % of profitable trades (60% is excellent)
- **Discipline Score**: % of trades following your system (90%+ is the real goal)

A 50% win rate trader with 100% discipline beats an 80% win rate trader with 0% discipline.

### Emotional States
You track 4 emotional states:
- **Calm** - Best for trading (aim for 80%+)
- **Excited** - Can lead to over-trading
- **Frustrated** - Should STOP trading immediately
- **Fearful** - Can cause hesitation on good setups

Pattern: If you see 30%+ frustrated trades, your system isn't working or you're not following it.

## Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Discipline Over Win Rate
A 40% win rate with strict discipline beats a 70% win rate with revenge trading.
**Keep your discipline score above 90%.**

### Tip 2: One Strategy at a Time
Pick ONE recommended strategy. Master it. Use it for at least 20 trades before switching.

### Tip 3: Emotional Awareness
You can't change your emotions, but you can control your trades.
- Trading frustrated? STOP.
- Trading excited? Reduce size.
- Trading calm? Full size.

### Tip 4: P&L Doesn't Matter (Yet)
Your first 30 trades don't matter for P&L. They matter for learning the system.

### Tip 5: Keep Notes
The "Trade Notes" field is where you learn. Write:
- What happened?
- Did I follow my system?
- What can I improve?

### Tip 6: Case Studies Are Textbooks
Real trades teach better than anything else. Study both wins AND losses.

## Troubleshooting

### Trades Disappeared
**Solution**: Check browser Settings → Storage. Don't clear app data.

### Can't Complete Checklist
**Solution**: Make sure all 10 items are checked. No partial completion allowed.

### Charts Not Showing
**Solution**: You need at least 3 closed trades. Log more trades.

### Mobile Nav Not Showing
**Solution**: View on phone, not desktop. Or use narrow browser window.

### App Won't Load
**Solution**: Check internet connection, clear cache, try incognito mode.

## Advanced Tips

### Position Sizing Formula
Risk per trade = Account Size × Risk %
Position Size = Risk per trade ÷ (Entry - Stop Loss)

Example:
- Account: $10,000
- Risk: 2% = $200
- Entry: 1.0850
- Stop: 1.0825 (25 pips)
- Position: 200 ÷ (25 × 10) = 0.8 lots

### Risk/Reward Minimum
- Never trade less than 1:2
- 1:3 is ideal
- 1:1 or worse = DON'T ENTER

### Session Selection
- London: 8:00-17:00 GMT (best volatility)
- New York: 13:00-22:00 GMT
- Tokyo: 0:00-9:00 GMT
- Sydney: 22:00 (prev)-7:00 GMT
